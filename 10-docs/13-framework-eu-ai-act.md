# Framework Deep Dive: EU AI Act

## Purpose
Summarize the EU AI Act context relevant to this repository and clarify boundaries for usage.

## What It Is
Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 provides a risk-based legal framework for AI in the European Union.

## Scope and Applicability
- Legal obligations vary by system risk category and role in the value chain.
- Applicability depends on deployment context and jurisdiction.
- Legal interpretation should be reviewed by qualified counsel.
