# AI Governance Charter (One Page)

## Charter Name
Enterprise TRiSM Charter for Framework Deployment

## Effective Date
2026-04-10

## Mission
Establish accountable and auditable trust, risk, and security governance for enterprise framework deployment across model, data, application, and operations lifecycle.

## Scope
- In scope: production and pre-production enterprise framework deployments.
- Control domains: trust, risk, security, privacy, compliance, and operational resilience.
- Out of scope: experimental sandboxes with no production data or user exposure.

## Guiding Principles
- Risk-based decisions with documented residual risk.
- Security and privacy by design.
- Evidence-driven deployment approvals.
- Clear ownership and review cadence.

## Objectives (Year 1)
- 100% of production releases pass TRiSM gate checks.
- 100% of high and critical risks have assigned treatment plans.
- Audit evidence package can be generated in less than 5 business days.

## Decision Rights
- Governance Board approves policy exceptions and high-risk go-live decisions.
- Domain owners approve controls in their accountable domains.
- Platform owner enforces technical gates in CI/CD workflows.

## Review Cadence
- Monthly TRiSM operations review.
- Quarterly policy and control matrix review.
- Per-release gate approval review.

## Escalation Path
1. Domain lead
2. TRiSM governance lead
3. Executive sponsor and governance board

## Document Control
- Owner: AI Governance Lead
- Reviewers: Security, Privacy, Model Risk, Platform Engineering, Internal Audit Liaison
