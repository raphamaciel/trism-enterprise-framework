# Executive Summary: AI TRiSM Governance-as-Code Repository

## What This Repository Is
An experimental, open-source governance-as-code repository for operationalizing AI trust, risk, and security practices.

It provides practical templates and workflows that help teams manage AI governance across design, deployment, runtime operations, and audit review.

## What It Is Not
- Not affiliated with or endorsed by Gartner.
- Not a certification program.
- Not a compliance guarantee.

## Why It Matters
Many organizations have policy documents but lack repeatable operational controls.

This repository turns governance intent into enforceable engineering practice through:
- standardized templates
- gate-based approvals
- pull request workflow controls
- auditable decision and exception logs

## Business Value
- Improves accountability through clear ownership and review cadence.
- Reduces governance gaps between model development and production operations.
- Strengthens traceability for risk, security, and privacy decisions.
- Supports faster internal assurance and audit-readiness.

## Operating Model
Governance is enforced through lifecycle gates and required evidence:
- Gate 1: Design Assurance
- Gate 2: Pre-Production Assurance
- Gate 3: Go-Live Assurance
- Gate 4: Post-Live Assurance

## Accountability Model
Decision-making and control ownership are distributed across governance, security, privacy, model risk, platform engineering, and audit stakeholders.

## How To Use
1. Adapt templates to your organizational risk profile.
2. Use pull requests as governance checkpoints.
3. Require designated approvers before merge.
4. Maintain decision and exception records with time-bound reviews.

## Reference Alignment
Repository artifacts are designed for practical alignment with:
- Gartner AI TRiSM (conceptual operating model reference)
- NIST AI RMF
- ISO/IEC 42001
- EU AI Act (where applicable)

Alignment is informational and operational, not a formal compliance claim.
